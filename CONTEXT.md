# Domain Context

## Glossary

- **Broker Account** — A linkage to a specific MetaTrader 5 (MT5) account. Can be in `PENDING` or `VERIFIED` status.
- **Ledger** — The immutable record of financial movements. Entries are either `CREDIT` (incoming) or `DEBIT` (outgoing).
- **Rebate** — A credit entry in the **Ledger** generated by trade volume on a linked **Broker Account**.
- **Withdrawal** — A debit entry in the **Ledger** requested by a user. Requires a **Ticket** for tracking and approval.
- **Ticket** — A workflow item representing a request that requires administrative action (e.g., `VERIFICATION` for accounts, `WITHDRAWAL` for funds).
- **Action Token** — A time-limited secure token used for sensitive flows like email verification or password reset.
